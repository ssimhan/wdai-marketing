Four things to notice

Signs a model is guessing.

Unusually specific numbers.
"73.4% of studies" with no citation is a tell.
Confident citations.
Real-sounding papers that don't exist.
Smoothed-over edges.
Counterarguments disappearing into "balance."
Answers on topics it can't know.
Events after the training cutoff, named unless asked.
